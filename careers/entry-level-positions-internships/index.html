About You

What We are Looking For

Day-to-day our Associates conduct research, develop industry knowledge and build their network. You’ll spend time proactively mapping your specialist sector and identifying key talent and rising stars within it. You’ll also assist with preparing client reports, search execution and business development work.

We are looking for a broad skillset and a go-getting attitude :
Commercial
You seek to understand how businesses operate and the drivers that lead them to succeed or fail
Analytical
You are thoughtful, enjoy research and problem-solving
Resilient
The ability to bounce back with solutions and positivity
Emotional Intelligence
This business is all about good judgement, understanding people and building relationships
Gravitas
You’ll need to successfully communicate and hold your own with senior people
A Team Player
Enjoy supporting others and collaborating to achieve common goals
