Industry Overview

Asset Owners

We have a long history of partnering with sovereign, quasi-sovereign, endowment and foundation, pension, charity and family office clients. Our clients invest permanent or long-term pools of capital on behalf of institutions and their beneficiaries, who ultimately rely on the success of the investment platform.

We have successfully completed investment and investment support searches for Board, C-level and senior professionals who are responsible for setting and delivering against the long-term goals of these organizations.

We can draw knowledge from our relevant functional practice areas and combine this with an understanding of the unique characteristics of these institutions and their long-term goals, to offer executive search services that are tailored to this client type.

Charlie Kershaw is a Partner at Dore Partnership in the New York office, where he leads the Private Equity practice group. Charlie focuses on hiring investors, operating partners, capital raisers and for senior leadership positions for investment firms and their portfolio companies.
