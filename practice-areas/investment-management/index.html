Industry Overview

Investment Management

The Investment Management and Asset Owners practice partners with Boards, Executive Management and wider firm leadership to deliver the advice and talent to help capture the opportunities and meet the challenges they face in a rapidly evolving global marketplace.

We have successfully completed searches for investment and business leadership, high impact investment professionals across all major discretionary and quantitative strategies, marketing and investor relations professionals, risk management, data science and technology professionals.

We support businesses in their digital transition and in the development of their public and private markets solutions platforms. Many of these engagements are highly confidential and/or facilitate growth into new strategies, geographies and capabilities, or support deeper engagement with specific LP segments.

Leveraging our deep understanding of both investment managers and asset owners accrued over many years of search and advisory experience, we partner with specialist colleagues to ensure our clients are supported by a team with domain and functional expertise in the relevant sector, strategy, role and region.

Arthur Barrington-Ward

London

Arthur is a Partner at Dore Partnership, based in the London office. He is responsible for working closely with clients in the delivery of senior investment, distribution and leadership searches across a broad array of products and geographies. He is also the firm’s ESG lead. Prior to joining Dore Partnership, he was a Partner and Head of the Asset Management Practice at Hutton Consulting in London.  He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Social Anthropology with Thai Language and Literature from the School of Oriental and African Studies, University of London. Arthur is also trained in conducting the Hogan personality and leadership assessment.

Rod Haire

Hong Kong

Rod is a Partner at Dore Partnership, responsible for working closely with clients in the delivery of senior leadership executives across a broad array of functions, sectors and geographies. Prior to joining Dore Partnership, he was Head of the Hong Kong office and Global Head of Insurance at Global Sage, and a Senior Client Partner at Korn Ferry.  Before moving into Executive Search, Rod had a long corporate career across banking and insurance in the UK, Middle East and Asia, including as in country CEO for multinational insurers, and has lived and worked in Bahrain, Pakistan, Indonesia, Vietnam, Singapore and Hong Kong. Rod holds both a Bachelor and Master of Arts in Economics from the University of Cambridge.
