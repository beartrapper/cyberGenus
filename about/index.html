About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to help great businesses and exceptional leaders succeed together.

Since 1997 we’ve been helping ambitious businesses and people achieve commercial success. We also look at the bigger picture and are careful to only partner with companies who share in our values and seek to impact their employees, stakeholders and the world in positive ways.

Our Values

We are one global team unified by a set of values that run through everything we do.

Entrepreneurial
We work with creativity and vision. Our workplace is meritocratic and opportunity rich.
Integrity
We use good judgement and don’t take shortcuts. We treat each other in a respectful and thoughtful way.
Disciplined
We have a strong work ethic and pursue the highest quality outcomes.
Diverse Thinking
We actively seek out different perspectives, backgrounds and thought processes.
Results Driven
We won’t rest until we’ve achieved success. We recognize and reward achievement.

Jason Ne Win

Principal

Jason is based in Dore Partnership’s Hong Kong office and is responsible for advising clients and executing searches in both the traditional and alternative asset management industries across Asia Pacific. Before moving back to Hong Kong in 2017, Jason spent over a year based in the firm’s New York office. Prior to joining Dore Partnership, he spent over three years at an international search firm, where he focused on hiring for Private Capital and Investment Banking clients in Asia.

He initially joined in Singapore before moving to Hong Kong to help set up and build their presence for North Asia. He holds a Bachelor in Business Administration from the National University of Singapore. Jason is also trained in conducting the Hogan personality and leadership assessment.

Charles Dore

CEO

Charlie is the founder and CEO of Dore Partnership, based in New York. He originally founded the firm in London in 1997, opening the Hong Kong and New York offices in 2004 respectively, and relocating with his family to New York in 2007. Diversity & Inclusion

Diversity and inclusion are integral to our success

Diversity and inclusion are at the heart of our day to day work. We purposefully identify a diverse candidate shortlist on every project and we help our clients build more diverse teams at board, executive and functional level.
In our company culture

We are proud to work alongside clients, candidates and colleagues who come from varied cultures, backgrounds and communities. We foster collaboration, intellectual curiosity and the exchange of different viewpoints.

In our search process

In our client work we use proven strategies to broaden the search, tap into diverse talent pools and monitor the candidate mix throughout the process. Our staff complete training in unconscious bias, and other techniques.

Within our sector

Within our sector we have strong relationships with professional organizations who drive diversity in the workplace such as 100 Women in Finance, Diversity VC, and Toigo.
